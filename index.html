Amenhotep IV
(Akhenaten)

Akhenaten; It means "the living spirit of the Aten" He was also known before the fifth year of his reign as Amenhotep IV. He was a pharaoh of the Eighteenth Dynasty who ruled Egypt for 17 years and died probably in 1336 BC or 1334 BC. He is noted for abandoning the plurality of traditional Egyptian deities and introducing a new cult centered on the Aten. And Akhenaten was married to his wife, the main queen, Nefertiti, who shared his idea of bworshiping the Aten and appeared with him in religious ceremonies and accompanied him in all his scenes......................
